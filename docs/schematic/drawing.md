# Drawing a Schematic

## Placing symbols

Press `A` or use the left toolbar to open the **symbol chooser**. Search by
name or browse the library tree, then click to place. Rotate with `R` before or
after placing.

## Wiring

Use the **Wire** tool (`W`) to connect pins. A **junction** dot appears
automatically where wires cross and connect. Free wire ends and unconnected
pins show a small dangling marker so you can spot gaps at a glance.

## Labels and power

- **Local / global / hierarchical labels** name nets and connect them by name.
- **Power symbols** (GND, +3V3, …) mark power nets.
- **No-connect** flags (`Q`) mark pins that are intentionally left unconnected.

## Editing

- `M` move, `G` drag (keeps wires attached), `R` rotate, `Del` delete.
- Double-click an item to edit its properties; the **Properties** panel edits
  the current selection live.
