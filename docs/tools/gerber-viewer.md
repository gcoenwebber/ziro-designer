# Gerber Viewer

The **Gerber Viewer** lets you inspect fabrication outputs — Gerber layers and
Excellon drill files — before you send them to a manufacturer. Load your output
set and step through layers to confirm the board is what you expect.

## Typical use

1. Generate Gerbers and drill files from the PCB Editor.
2. Open them in the Gerber Viewer.
3. Toggle layers, check drill marks, and verify alignment and coverage.
