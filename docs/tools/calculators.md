# Calculators

Ziro Designer includes the engineering **calculators** you reach for during
design — a faithful port of the classic PCB calculator toolset.

## Included calculators

- **Regulators** — linear and adjustable regulator resistor values.
- **Transmission lines** — microstrip, striplines (incl. coupled), coplanar,
  with substrate presets and dielectric dispersion.
- **Track width** and **electrical spacing** for current and voltage.
- **Fusing current**, **cable size**, and **RF attenuators**.
- **Resistor substitution** across the E-series.

Results and formulas are matched against reference implementations, with the QA
suite carrying the original test vectors.
