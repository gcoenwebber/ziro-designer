# Introduction to Ziro Designer

**Ziro Designer** is the browser-native, open-source PCB engineering suite from
[ZIRO EDA](https://www.ziroeda.com). It brings a full, KiCad-class hardware
design workflow — schematic capture, PCB layout, Gerber viewing, and
engineering calculators — to the web, with nothing to install.

Open a browser, and you have a complete electronics design environment.

## Why browser-native?

- **No installation.** The entire suite runs in the browser. Your projects live
  in the cloud and follow you between machines.
- **Open source.** Ziro Designer is released under the GPL-3.0-or-later license.
  The engine is a faithful, from-scratch port of proven EDA workflows.
- **One continuous workflow.** Schematic → layout → manufacturing outputs, all
  in the same tab, sharing one project model.

## What's inside

| Editor | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Schematic Editor** | Capture circuits, place symbols, wire nets, annotate. |
| **PCB Editor** | Place footprints, route tracks and vias, pour zones, run checks. |
| **Gerber Viewer** | Inspect fabrication outputs before you send them out. |
| **Calculators** | Trace width, transmission lines, regulators, resistor E-series, and more. |
| **Image Converter** | Turn logos and artwork into silkscreen or copper footprints. |
